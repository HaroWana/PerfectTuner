PerfectTuner — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PerfectTuner handles your information. The short version: we collect nothing. PerfectTuner is a guitar tuning app that processes audio entirely on your device and transmits nothing to any server.

What Data We Collect

None. PerfectTuner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or device identifiers. No data of any kind leaves your device as a result of using this app.

Microphone Use

PerfectTuner requests access to your device's microphone exclusively to detect the pitch of audio signals for guitar tuning. Audio is processed entirely on-device in real time.

  • No audio is ever recorded to storage.
  • No audio is ever transmitted off the device.
  • Microphone access is only active while the tuner screen is in the foreground.

No Advertising

PerfectTuner contains no advertising of any kind and includes no advertising SDKs. You will never see ads in this app.

No Analytics

PerfectTuner does not use any analytics or crash-reporting service. No usage events, session data, or diagnostic information is collected or transmitted.

No User Accounts

PerfectTuner does not require or support user accounts. No email address, username, or any form of personal identification is requested or stored.

In-App Purchases

PerfectTuner offers one optional in-app purchase: unlocking the full tuning library. This transaction is processed entirely through Google Play Billing. PerfectTuner does not handle, store, or have access to any payment information. The app only receives a confirmation of whether the purchase has been completed.

Google's privacy policy governs the billing transaction: policies.google.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party component used by PerfectTuner is the Google Play Billing Library, used solely for in-app purchase processing as described above. No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are present in the app.

Children

PerfectTuner does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, there is no special handling required for younger users.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: privacy@perfecttuner.app
